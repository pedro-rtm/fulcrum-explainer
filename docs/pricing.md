## Year 1 Financial Model

- 2 DD engagements × GBP 12.5K avg = GBP 25K
- 1 post-deal execution × GBP 30K = GBP 30K
- 20 expert network calls × GBP 750 avg = GBP 15K

## Pricing Psychology Notes

- Never quote hourly rates for DD work. Always quote project-based: "A full technology DD is GBP [X]K, delivered in 10–15 working days"
- The cost-to-fix estimate in the DD report often identifies GBP 500K–5M+ of technology risk/opportunity. Pedro's GBP 15–30K fee is <5% of the value identified. Frame pricing this way
- PE firms are not price-sensitive on DD (it's <0.5% of deal value on a GBP 50M+ acquisition). They are quality-sensitive and speed-sensitive
- Expert network calls are the bridge income while DD pipeline builds. GBP 500–2K for a 1-hour call is excellent ROI on time
