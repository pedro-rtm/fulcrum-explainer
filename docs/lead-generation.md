## Layer 3: LinkedIn (highest volume) — Week 2+

### Content cadence: 2–3 posts/week

| Day | Post type | Example |
|-----|-----------|---------|
| Monday | Insight post | "Across 80+ technology assessments, the #1 risk I've seen is [X]. Here's why PE firms miss it" |
| Wednesday | Metric/case study | "A PE fund asked what a legacy ERP migration would cost. Here's how I estimated GBP 2.4M" |
| Friday | Question/engagement | "What's the most common tech risk you've seen kill a deal?" |

### Post formats that work for PE DD
1. **"What I'd check" posts** — "If I were doing tech DD on a [company type], here are the 5 things I'd look at first"
2. **Pattern posts** — "After 80+ technology assessments, the pattern is clear: [insight]"
3. **Cost quantification posts** — "How I estimated GBP [X] of technical debt in 3 days"
4. **IC memo posts** — "What an IC member actually needs from a tech DD report (it's not what you think)"
5. **Contrarian takes** — "Most tech DD reports are useless. Here's what actually helps make the investment decision"

### DM outreach: 5–10/day

- Sales Navigator filter: "operating partner" OR "portfolio operations" AND "technology" in UK
- Message template: personalized, reference their fund/portco, offer insight not pitch

### Substack: 1 article/week

- DD frameworks, pattern analysis, case studies
- Cross-post to Medium for SEO backstop
