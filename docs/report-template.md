## Appendix A: Methodology & Data Sources

### Assessment Methodology

This report follows the Fulcrum 7-Dimension Technology DD Framework:

1. **Architecture & Infrastructure** — system topology, cloud maturity, technical debt, scalability
2. **Team & Organization** — headcount, composition, key-person risk, hiring plan
3. **Security & Compliance** — OWASP, SOC 2, GDPR/NIS2, incident history
4. **Data & Analytics** — quality, governance, integration, AI readiness
5. **Cost Structure** — spend analysis, optimization, benchmarking, cost-to-fix
6. **Delivery Capability** — DORA metrics, DevOps maturity, backlog health
7. **Strategic Alignment** — roadmap fit, M&A readiness, competitive moat

### Scoring Scale

| Score | Label | Definition |
|:-----:|-------|-----------|
| 1 | Critical | Immediate risk to operations. Emergency intervention required |
| 2 | Significant | Major gaps impacting value creation. 6-month remediation |
| 3 | Moderate | Functional but suboptimal. 12-month improvement roadmap |
| 4 | Good | Minor gaps. Optimization opportunities exist |
| 5 | Excellent | Best-in-class for company size/stage |
